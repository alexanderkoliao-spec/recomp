Post-Duty Day Contingency

If prior day was >8hr OR / on-call / late night surgery
🔄
Downgrade that session to 60-70% volume: same exercises, drop top sets, reduce load 10-15%, skip the finisher. RPE ceiling 7.
🔄
If heavy lower day (Thu/Sat) falls post-duty: swap to upper accessory + mobility (15 min face pulls/band work + 20 min walk + stretch). Reschedule lower to Friday or Sunday if possible.
🔄
RHR >+7 bpm over 7d avg for 3 days: same downgrade. Two consecutive downgrades = full rest day.
⚕️
Nutrition unchanged — don't compensate down. Protein floor still 200g. Post-duty days are when LBM loss risk spikes.

Cardio & Progression

🚴
Post-session cardio: 20-30 min incline walk or stationary bike. No running, no plyometrics.
📈
Progression: Add 1 rep/set before adding weight. Hit top of rep range all sets → add ~2.5kg.
🎯
RPE: Compounds = 7-8. Accessories = 8-9. Never to failure on compound lifts.
🦵
L knee effusion present: Skip all knee-dominant work. Upper + hip hinge only that day.
💪
R shoulder flare: Cable-only upper, reduce ROM, swap DB press for machine chest press.
⏸️
Deload at week 6: 5 days, 60% volume, same frequency. Prevents CNS burnout on 900 kcal deficit.

Vitamin K2 (MK-7) ADD
180 mcg MK-7 daily (Knapen 3-year RCT dose)
Morning with D3 + fat-containing meal. MK-7 half-life is ~72 hours — once daily is enough.
iHerb pick: Life Extension Super K — MK-7 180 mcg + MK-4 1000 mcg + K1 1500 mcg in one softgel. 90 softgels = 3 months.

⚠ Critical: do NOT buy Thorne Vitamin D + K2 Liquid. Despite the elegant all-in-one format, Thorne's K2 is MK-4 (menatetrenone), not MK-7. MK-4 at 200 mcg is pharmacologically inert:
• Sato et al. 2012 (Nutrition J.): at 420 mcg oral MK-4, serum K2 was undetectable at every time point in every subject
• MK-4 half-life = 1-4 hours; the Japanese osteoporosis doses that worked were 45mg (= 45,000 mcg) 3×/day — pharmacological, not nutritional
• MK-7 half-life = 72 hours, and the Knapen RCT showed 180 mcg MK-7 daily significantly improved BMD and arterial calcification biomarkers over 3 years

The Thorne liquid essentially delivers D3 alone with an inert companion. Split D3 and K2 into two products instead — the Life Extension Super K above is the MK-7 answer.

Alternative single-product combo: Sports Research Vitamin D3 + K2 (5000 IU + 100 mcg MK-7) — on iHerb, real MK-7 form. Only downside: fixed D3 dose means you can't titrate by serum 25-OH-D without double-dosing.
